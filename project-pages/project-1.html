VFX - SKY MONSTER (Stranger Things)
Developed a Sky Monster VFX inspired by 'Stranger Things' using After Effects. This project highlights my expertise in visual effects and creative design, bringing a cinematic and eerie atmosphere to life
Project Details / Background
For this project, I created a Sky Monster visual effect inspired by 'Stranger Things' using After Effects. This involved designing a menacing and atmospheric creature that seamlessly integrates with the sky, evoking a sense of suspense and mystery. In addition to the visual effects, I also handled the sound design using Premiere Pro, crafting an immersive audio experience that complements the eerie visuals. This project showcases my skills in both VFX and sound design, demonstrating my ability to create compelling and cinematic scenes that captivate and engage the audience.
